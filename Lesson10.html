Lesson 10

Virtual Trip

2

2

There are many locations in Japan that one would want to visit. There are landmarks, historic temples, and many other places that are exclusive to Japan.

2

2

2

This lesson is about planning a virtual trip or tour of Japan. Each person should find at least two locations that they would like to visit and talk about them with this club. A picture or two would be nice as well to make the virtual trip more interesting.

2

If you are doing this solo then try and research a number of different locations and tell someone about it. It could be your parents or grandparents or maybe even your friend, just share it with them and have a fun time learning about the many places in Japan.
