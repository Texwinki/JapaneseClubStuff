Lesson 6

Simple conversations

2

2

This lesson will allow you to use your knowledge you’ve gained this far to write your own sentences. These will be relatively short and basic, but make sure to be creative. If you want to use a word that wasn’t taught in this club, then use websites that aren’t translators. It’s more accurate to find example sentences and work from there.

2

2

2

Write a simple 2-4 sentence conversation between you and a partner, if you are doing this alone then do it alone. Make sure to use at least 1 verb and at least 1 adjective. Use previous vocab lists to write them, but make sure you’re using the correct particles. I would suggest writing your sentences in romaji, if you feel confident enough in your hiragana you can write it in that instead.
