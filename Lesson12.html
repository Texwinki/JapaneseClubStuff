Well, it's about that time, the end of the website. I hope you learned something through these lessons and I hope you enjoyed it as well. This was just a brief introduction to Japanese for those who think they may be interested in learning it further.

2

If you want to further your Japanese education I’d suggest starting with Duolingo, its an app that helps memorise certain words and characters. Duolingo isn’t the best for learning the language well, you need to do more if you want to be fluent.

2

If you have the time, I'd fully recommend taking Japanese 1, 2 and 3 provided by NCVPS. I took all of them as my language credits for High school and I enjoyed it. All of the information in this site comes from that curriculum.

2

Finally i'd recommend anime, it's quite cool, and obscure. Yep.

2

All jokes aside, watching anime with english subtitles is helpful for learning how to talk. If you want recommendations off learning japanese I’d suggest children's anime like DOREMON, or if you like a lot of talking I’d recommend Death Note. Though Death Note is pg 13 and goes into some dark topics so be warned. OOOOOOOOOOOO.
